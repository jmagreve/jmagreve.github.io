Hypnotherapy


is a goal-oriented self-improvement technique that mobilize the power of our minds toward the achievement of a goal. Hypnosis is a natural, yet altered state of mind characterized by mental and physical relaxation. It is within natural human capacity to be able to learn hypnosis, and hypnosis is really more of a verb than a noun - it is something we do, and we do it often, quite naturally. Watching TV, driving down the highway, drifting off into a pleasant day dream, these are all altered states of consciousness that we move in and out of throughout each day.


The beauty of learning hypnosis is that it is a state of mind where the conscious, analytical mind is relaxed, and there is a unique opportunity to communicating directly with the subconscious mind, wherein we can alter beliefs, heal the past, learn new and resourceful skills and ways of thinking, or direct healing commands to our bodies.


Hypnotherapy is a cooperative venture, a partnership, between you and I. As such, I would like to refer to you as my co-hypnotherapist. Together, we will search for solutions, prepare suggestions, and develop imagery that will facilitate the achievement of your goal, whether it be financial, relational, career or health.
